Gamenest Rules & Policy

1. Respect and Sportsmanship

- **Fair Play**: All players are expected to demonstrate good sportsmanship. Cheating, exploiting bugs, or using unfair tactics will not be tolerated.

- **Respect Others**: We foster a community of kindness and respect. Harassment, offensive language, and bullying are strictly prohibited.

- **No Toxic Behavior**: Negative or abusive behavior, including trolling, flaming, or griefing, is not allowed. Everyone should feel safe and welcome.

2. Account Security

- **Personal Information**: Protect your account credentials. Do not share your login details with anyone. Gamnest is not responsible for any account theft or misuse.

- **Password Policy**: Use a strong and unique password to keep your account secure. Avoid using easily guessable passwords like your name or birthdate.

- **Account Sharing**: Do not share your account with others. Each player should only use their personal account to ensure fair competition.

3. In-Game Conduct

- **No Exploiting**: Using third-party programs or hacks to gain an advantage is strictly prohibited. Players found using cheats will be banned.

- **Appropriate Content**: Any offensive, inappropriate, or explicit content within the arcade will be removed. This includes usernames, avatars, and chat content.

- **Game Mods**: Any modification or tampering with the game files is against the rules. Only use official tools and updates provided by Gamnest.

4. Payment and Virtual Goods

- **Purchases**: Gamnest Arcade may offer virtual goods, in-game currencies, and premium content for purchase. All transactions are final and non-refundable unless specified by our refund policy.

- **No Unauthorized Transactions**: Do not engage in unauthorized transactions for in-game currency or goods. Only purchase items from the official Gamnest platform.

- **Payment Security**: All payments should be made through authorized and secure payment methods. Ensure your payment details are protected.

5. Community and Communication

- **Chat Use**: Players are encouraged to communicate and interact, but all communication should remain respectful. Offensive language or spamming is prohibited.

- **Reports and Feedback**: If you encounter any players violating the rules or experience technical issues, please report it through our support system. We take all feedback seriously to improve the arcade experience.

- **Social Media**: When posting about Gamnest on social media, always represent the brand respectfully. We love our community and appreciate positive engagement!

6. Arcade Environment

- **Safe Space**: Gamnest is committed to being a safe and inclusive environment for players of all ages. We do not tolerate discrimination, harassment, or hate speech of any kind.

- **Underage Players**: If you are under the age of 13, you must have permission from a parent or guardian to use our services.

- **Moderation and Reporting**: Our moderation team is actively monitoring the arcade to ensure that all players follow the rules. If you observe inappropriate behavior, please report it immediately.

7. Violations and Penalties

- **Warnings**: Minor infractions will result in a warning. Repeated offenses may lead to temporary or permanent bans.

- **Suspension or Ban**: Severe violations, including cheating, exploiting, or harmful behavior, will result in account suspension or permanent banning from the arcade.

- **Appeals**: If you feel your account was banned or suspended unfairly, you may submit an appeal. We will review each case thoroughly and fairly.

8. Privacy and Data Protection
